Idaptive API Demo
A demonstration of the power of integrating with the Centrify Identify Platform.

The Centrify Identity Platform (CIP) is the power behind many popular Centrify products such as Centrify Application Services and Centrify Infrastructure Services. CIP is not just available for Centrify products to use, however, which is why every powerful feature of the platform is available to developers via RESTful API's. Every feature of Centrify Application Services can be integrated into your own websites and products. MFA can be integrated into your own applications. Identity management can be simplified for your customer facing products and websites. The possibilities are many with the Centrify Identity Platform.

The purpose of this demonstration is to show a few of the integration possibilities of the Centrify Identity Platform. This entire site has been programmed in ASP.Net and C#, and every page is independent. This demo is completely open source and can be used in integration projects or simply to learn. The source code can be found on Github. To learn more about the Centrify Identity Platform API's, and how to use them, please visit the Centrify Developer Portal.
